## CORE PRICING STRUCTURE

### Daily Rate Structure

**Field Service (On-Site):** $7,500/day (Standard) | $15,000/day (Ultra-Premium)
- Records digitization at aircraft location
- 3D scanning operations (exterior, interior, mechanical)
- Equipment transport and setup
- Original document handling and return
- On-site quality verification

**Post-Production:** $3,400/day (Standard) | $5,000/day (Ultra-Premium)
- Point cloud processing and optimization
- OCR and metadata tagging
- Quality control and verification
- Client deliverable preparation
- RecordVault platform upload and configuration
- **Ratio: 3 post-production days per 1 field day**

**Travel Expenses:** At cost + per diem
- Airfare: Actual cost (economy class domestic, business class international)
- Ground transportation: Actual cost (rental car, Uber, taxi)
- Lodging: Actual cost (reasonable business-class hotels)
- Meals: Per diem at federal GSA rates ($79/day domestic, $100/day international)
- Equipment shipping (if required): Actual cost
- International fees (visas, customs, etc.): Actual cost

**Per Diem Daily Rate:** $2,400/day (portal-to-portal travel time)
- Covers technician time while traveling
- Does NOT include actual travel expenses (see above)

## TRAVEL EXPENSE BILLING METHODOLOGY

### Transparent Cost-Plus Model

**What We Bill:**
1. **Actual documented costs** (receipts provided):
   - Airfare (economy domestic, business international)
   - Hotel (business-class properties)
   - Ground transportation (rental cars, rideshare)
   - Parking and tolls
   - Equipment shipping (if required)
   - Visas, customs fees, international logistics

2. **Per diem at GSA rates** (no receipts required):
   - Domestic: $79/day for meals and incidentals
   - International: $100/day for meals and incidentals

3. **Travel time daily rate** ($2,400/day):
   - Portal-to-portal travel days
   - Includes technician time while in transit
   - Does NOT include actual travel expenses (billed separately)

**What We Don't Bill:**
- Markup on travel expenses (billed at actual cost)
- Personal expenses
- Non-business activities

**Example Breakdown (Florida to Texas for Tier 2):**
```
Base Service: $35,400
  - Field service (2 days): $15,000
  - Post-production (6 days): $20,400

Travel Expenses (at cost):
  - Roundtrip airfare (DFW-MCO): $450
  - Rental car (3 days): $180
  - Hotel (2 nights): $320
  - Per diem meals (2 days × $79): $158
  - Subtotal actual expenses: $1,108

Travel Time:
  - Per diem (1 day travel × $2,400): $2,400

TOTAL INVESTMENT: $38,908
```

## PAYMENT TERMS & BILLING

### Deposit & Payment Schedule

**50% Deposit Upon Booking:**
- Reserves field service date
- Covers initial equipment/travel procurement
- Refundable per cancellation policy (see below)

**Travel Expense Billing:**
- Option A: Include estimated travel in 50% deposit, reconcile at completion
- Option B: Bill actual travel expenses separately upon completion (with receipts)

**50% Balance Upon Delivery:**
- Due when final deliverables are provided
- Includes any travel expense reconciliation (if Option A selected)

### Payment Methods
- Wire transfer (preferred for international clients)
- ACH/bank transfer
- Corporate check
- Credit card (3% processing fee applies to amounts >$10,000)

### Cancellation Policy
- **30+ days notice:** Full refund minus $1,000 administrative fee
- **15-29 days notice:** 50% refund (we've committed field resources)
- **14 days or less:** No refund (travel booked, schedule blocked)
- **Weather/mechanical delays:** Reschedule at no penalty, travel expenses may apply if already incurred
