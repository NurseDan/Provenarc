**"That seems expensive."**
→ "Compared to what? Lost logbooks cost $50K-$500K to reconstruct. Missing records reduce resale value 15-30%. Our service is insurance, not an expense."

**"Can I get a discount?"**
→ "Our pricing reflects global concierge delivery—we come to you anywhere in the world. If you have multiple aircraft at the same location, we offer fleet discounts up to 15%."

**"Why not just use [Competitor]?"**
→ "Great question. [Competitor] requires you to ship irreplaceable documents via FedEx—which loses thousands of packages daily. We eliminate that risk entirely. Plus, we're the only company offering integrated 3D scanning in the same visit."

**"Do I really need the 3D scanning?"**
→ "It depends on your goals. If you're selling, 3D scans let buyers inspect remotely before traveling—shortens your sales cycle. If you're insuring, baseline scans cut claims processing time in half. Not required, but highly valuable on aircraft over $500K."

**"Can I do Tier 1 now and add 3D later?"**
→ "Absolutely. You can always add standalone 3D scanning later for $8,850 per scan type plus travel. Bundling saves you money, but we're flexible."
