## Usage Guidelines

### In Legal Documents

Always use full legal names:

- “Provenarc Group LLC” (parent company)
- “Provenarc Aero Solutions” (aviation subsidiary)
- “Provenarc Marine” (marine subsidiary)

### In Marketing Materials

The subsidiary names ARE the brands. Use them directly:

- “Provenarc Aero Solutions provides professional aircraft records digitization…”
- “Provenarc Marine offers comprehensive vessel documentation…”

### In Customer Communications

Use the appropriate subsidiary name based on the service:

- Aviation clients → Provenarc Aero Solutions
- Marine clients → Provenarc Marine

### In Internal Communications

- Refer to the parent company as “Provenarc Group” or “the Company”
- Refer to subsidiaries by their full names or shortened versions:
  - “Aero Solutions” (internal shorthand acceptable)
  - “Marine” (internal shorthand acceptable)

## Brand Architecture Principles

1. **Clarity:** The subsidiary name IS the brand. No separate brand names.
1. **Scalability:** New divisions follow the “Provenarc [Industry]” naming pattern
1. **Legal Protection:** All subsidiary names protected via trademark
1. **Consistency:** Use full names in external materials; shortened versions acceptable internally only
