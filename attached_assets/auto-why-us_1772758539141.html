3D Documentation for Collectors

Beyond traditional provenance records, we offer comprehensive 3D documentation that captures your vehicle's physical condition and unique characteristics with precision.

Baseline Condition Documentation

High-resolution 3D scans create permanent records of your vehicle's condition at a specific point in time. This baseline documentation is invaluable for insurance, restoration planning, and demonstrating proper care to future buyers.

Restoration Progress Documentation

For vehicles undergoing restoration, we document before, during, and after conditions with 3D scanning. This comprehensive record proves the scope and quality of restoration work, supporting higher valuations and registry submissions.

Modification and Customization Records

Custom builds and modified vehicles benefit from detailed 3D documentation showing fabrication work, component integration, and finished results. This visual record complements written documentation of modifications.

Insurance and Authentication

Insurance carriers value baseline 3D scans for agreed-value policies and claims documentation. Authentication experts and auction houses use our documentation to verify originality and assess condition remotely.
