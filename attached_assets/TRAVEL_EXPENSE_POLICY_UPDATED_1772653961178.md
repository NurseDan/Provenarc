## CORE TRAVEL PHILOSOPHY

**We Travel at the Level of Service We Provide**

Provenarc Aero Solutions is a premium global concierge service. Our travel standards reflect:
- Professional presentation and client perception
- Technician rest and performance (long-haul international requires business class)
- Speed and flexibility (charter when expedited service demands it)
- Equipment transport requirements (3D scanning equipment is bulky/fragile)

**Bottom line:** We don't fly economy. Our team and equipment deserve better, and our clients expect it.

---

## TRAVEL CLASS STANDARDS

### Domestic Travel (Continental U.S.)

**Standard Service (Non-Expedited):**
- **Business Class minimum on all flights**
- Exception: Regional flights <2 hours may use premium economy if business unavailable
- Reasoning: Technician arrives rested, professional appearance, equipment carry-on privileges

**Expedited Service (Priority timing required):**
- **Private Charter if commercial routing adds >4 hours to total travel time**
- **Business Class minimum on commercial if charter unnecessary**
- Reasoning: Transaction deadlines are non-negotiable; speed justifies charter cost

### International Travel

**All International Flights:**
- **First Class standard** (all destinations, all service tiers)
- Long-haul (>6 hours): First Class non-negotiable
- Regional international (<3 hours): Business Class acceptable if First Class unavailable

**Expedited International Service:**
- **Private Charter preferred** if commercial routing adds significant time
- **First Class on commercial** if charter not operationally necessary
- Reasoning: International expedited service commands premium pricing; First Class reflects premium positioning

## PRIVATE CHARTER USAGE CRITERIA

### When Charter is Justified

**Expedited Service Requirements:**
1. **Time savings >4 hours** vs. best commercial routing
2. **Multiple legs required** on commercial (increases delay risk)
3. **Remote location** with limited/no commercial service
4. **Equipment transport urgency** (3D scanning gear, large volume records)
5. **Client requests charter** and agrees to cover actual cost

**Operational Requirements:**
1. **Weekend/holiday travel** when commercial options limited
2. **Multi-site itinerary** (e.g., document 3 aircraft in 3 cities in 5 days)
3. **Equipment volume** exceeds commercial baggage limits

**NOT Justified:**
- Standard service timelines (book commercial in advance)
- Single-destination trips with good commercial routing
- Domestic short-haul with frequent commercial flights
- Cost savings over business class <$5,000 (not worth complexity)

### Charter Cost Structure

**Light Jet (4-6 passengers, <1,500nm):**
- Hourly rate: $3,500-$5,000/hour
- Typical mission: 2-4 hours flight time
- **Example cost:** $10,000-$20,000 one-way

**Midsize Jet (6-8 passengers, 1,500-3,000nm):**
- Hourly rate: $5,000-$7,500/hour
- Typical mission: 3-6 hours flight time
- **Example cost:** $20,000-$45,000 one-way

**Heavy Jet (8-12 passengers, >3,000nm):**
- Hourly rate: $8,000-$12,000/hour
- Typical mission: 6-12 hours flight time
- **Example cost:** $50,000-$150,000 one-way

**Charter is billed at actual cost (no markup) + any positioning/deadhead fees**

## TRAVEL EXPENSE BILLING (REVISED)

### What We Bill at Actual Cost

**Airfare:**
- **Business class minimum** on all domestic commercial flights
- **First class standard** on all international commercial flights
- **Private charter** when justified by speed/logistics (actual invoice + positioning)

**Ground Transportation:**
- Rental car: Mid-size or larger (equipment transport)
- Rideshare/taxi: Business-class service (Uber Black, executive car service)
- Parking: Actual cost

**Lodging:**
- Business-class hotels (Marriott, Hilton, Hyatt properties)
- $200-$400/night domestic, $300-$600/night international
- Proximity to aircraft location prioritized (reduce drive time)

**Meals & Incidentals:**
- Per diem at GSA rates ($79/day domestic, $100/day international)
- No receipts required for per diem

**Equipment Shipping:**
- Oversized/fragile 3D scanning equipment
- Pelican cases, protective packaging
- Expedited shipping when timeline requires

**International Fees:**
- Visas, customs fees, import/export documentation
- Equipment carnets (temporary import permits)
- International driver's licenses

**Per Diem for Travel Time:**
- $2,400/day (portal-to-portal)
- Covers technician time in transit
- Does NOT include actual travel expenses (billed separately)

## CHARTER AUTHORIZATION PROCESS

### Step 1: Operational Assessment
- Commercial routing available?
- Time difference between charter and commercial?
- Does time savings justify charter cost?

### Step 2: Client Communication
- Explain charter cost vs. business class
- Present time savings and value justification
- Get explicit client authorization for charter expense

### Step 3: Internal Approval
- Charter <$30,000: Field manager approval
- Charter $30,000-$100,000: Co-managing member approval
- Charter >$100,000: Both co-managing members approval

### Step 4: Quote & Book
- Get firm charter quote (all-in with positioning)
- Present to client with comparison to commercial
- Client signs charter authorization form
- Book charter only after client approval

### Step 5: Expense Documentation
- Charter invoice attached to client billing
- Explanation memo included (time savings, justification)
- Transparency maintained (no markup on charter)
